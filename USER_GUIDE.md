# CivicPulse AI - User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to CivicPulse AI! This guide will help you get started with reporting civic issues and tracking their progress.

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit [civicpulse.kingston.ai](https://civicpulse.kingston.ai)
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile (optional but recommended)

## Reporting an Issue

1. Click the **microphone button** ðŸŽ¤
2. Speak your issue clearly:
   - *"There's a large pothole on Princess Street near the university"*
   - *"Broken streetlight on King Street West"*
   - *"Snow not cleared on my sidewalk at 123 Main Street"*
3. Review the transcription
4. Click **"Submit"** to report

**Tips for Best Results:**
- Speak clearly and at a normal pace
- Mention the location specifically
- Describe the issue in one sentence
- Include urgency keywords if needed ("dangerous", "urgent")

### Method 2: Text Input

1. Type your issue in the text box
2. Include location and description
3. Click **"Send"** or press Enter

## Privacy & Data

### What We Store
- Your reports (transcript, location, timestamp)
- Account information (email, preferences)
- Anonymized usage data

### What We DON'T Store
- Voice recordings (deleted after 5 seconds)
- Precise GPS coordinates (only intersection-level)
- Personal conversations
- Browsing history

### Data Retention
- Active reports: Until resolved + 30 days
- Resolved reports: 1 year for analytics
- Account data: Until you delete your account

## FAQs

**Q: How long does it take to report an issue?**  
A: With voice input, about 12 seconds on average (vs. 8 minutes with traditional forms).

**Q: Can I report anonymously?**  
A: Yes! Toggle "Anonymous Report" in settings.

**Q: What if the AI misunderstands my voice?**  
A: You can always edit the transcript before submitting or use text input instead.

**Q: Can I track someone else's report?**  
A: Yes, all reports are public (unless anonymous). Search by location or issue type.

**Q: How do I delete a report?**  
A: Contact support at support@civicpulse.ai within 24 hours of submission.

**Q: Is there a cost to use CivicPulse?**  
A: No, it's completely free for Kingston residents.
